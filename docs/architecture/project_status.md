## Current Frozen Analysis Snapshot

- Unified cohort: 2,247 retained genomes from 30 geopolitical countries, with 1,194 interpretable `prn` calls and 577 disrupted genomes.
- Genome catalog and FASTA provenance now resolves all 2,406 manifest rows through the formal registries, with 2,247 public assemblies backed by `bp_step2_public_genome_paths_qc` and 159 raw assemblies backed by `bp_step1_raw_read_step3_genome_paths`; the refreshed public path registry now reports 2,247 hits and 0 misses, and path resolution remains complete (`unresolved=0`).
- Standardized typing layer is now active ahead of the PRN structural layer: Step2 emits `modules/step2_typing/outputs/bp_genotype_manifest.tsv`, the unified manifest consumes harmonized `ptxP`/`fim3`/`fhaB2400_5550`/`23S` labels plus background profiles, and manuscript-facing genotype exports now live under `manuscript/figure_data/genotype_manifest.tsv`.
- Rooted repeated-emergence layer: the composition-pruned primary ASR quality frame retains 1,919 tips including 571 disrupted tips and 23 Fitch origins; support-threshold, country-balanced and study-block-balanced scenarios preserve repeated-origin support, with stochastic mapping retaining origin-count mass above one in all registered frames.
- Selected-country redesign: the submission-facing default screen now separates a Stage 1 primary epidemiologic set from a Stage 2 mechanistically triangulated subset. The default Stage 1 set is `USA`, `NZL`, `AUS`, `GBR`, and `JPN`; the default Stage 2 subset is `USA`, `NZL`, `AUS`, and `JPN`, with `GBR` retained as the explicit Stage-1-only comparator.
- Main manuscript identity: the paper is now framed for Communications Biology as a denominator-aware structural model of recurrent genome-defined *prn* disruption in public *Bordetella pertussis* genomes.
- Mechanism bridge remains intact but explicitly bounded: repeated origin is now anchored by the ASR scenario registry, constrained structural reuse, and same-country validation anchors, while local rooted reruns are retained as audit context rather than as standalone decisive anchors.
- Analysis-upgrade sidecars are now active: the selected-country leave-one-year-out layer, country-by-epoch architecture turnover, origin-burden versus prevalence-shift bridge, PRN-specificity negative-control audit, expanded interpretability diagnostics, multiverse readiness summaries, doubly robust contrasts, and ASR scenario registry are all generated from `manuscript/scripts/sidecars/ms_16_build_analysis_upgrade_sidecars.py`.
- Submission contract sync now runs `ms_15` and `ms_16` before `ms_14`, so the source-data manifest lands after the selected-country and sidecar freezes.
- Support-only sidecars remain active but bounded: the formulation-aware ecology layer still rests on 29 country-year rows from 9 countries, and the USA monthly focal model remains a descriptive Extended Data sidecar rather than a headline result.
- Static figure layer: the five main figures plus ten Supplementary Figure source panels complete under the current contract.
- Review-report reorganization: the old `manuscript/review report/` working area has been retired; active rebuild notes and selected-country TSVs now live in dedicated manuscript/docs directories.

## Current Risks And Open Work

- Step4 read validation remains partial and should still not be described as cohort-wide concordance.
- The selected-country comparison is intentionally asymmetric: `NZL` is the cleanest stable positive PRN-exposed anchor, `USA` remains positive but is more sensitivity-bearing under overlap-weighted missingness stress tests, `JPN` is triangulated but bounded PRN-free support, `GBR` is epidemiologically estimable but not triangulated, and `AUS` remains directionally discordant and low-count.
- The ecology sidecar and the USA focal-country model remain manuscript-supporting layers only; neither should be promoted back into the main claim hierarchy.
- The new manuscript-facing sidecars have been consolidated; future cleanup should continue to remove any outputs with no remaining consumers.
- The Communications Biology submission still needs final author metadata, public repository URL and archived DOI insertion, and completion of the official Reporting Summary and Editorial Policy Checklist forms.
